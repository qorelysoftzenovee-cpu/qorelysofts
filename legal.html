Terms of Service

Qorelysofts operates the indapp store as a curated platform for utility applications. We maintain a strict content policy that prioritizes legitimate, purpose-built software and enforces compliance with applicable Indian technology regulations. Users accessing the platform agree to the terms of service governing access, content use, and the distribution of verified application binaries.

The indapp store does not sell user data. We collect data only as necessary to provide, secure, and improve the service, and we retain it in accordance with our published retention policies and legal requirements. All interactions on the platform are subject to our standard usage terms and the Terms of Service contained herein.

Privacy Policy

Qorelysofts is committed to protecting user privacy and aligning with Indian data localization laws. Data collected through the indapp store is managed responsibly and only accessed for supporting essential platform features, security, and service improvements.

We do not monetise user information through sale or transfer, and we apply robust administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect user data. Any data transfers or disclosures are executed in compliance with applicable statutes, including the Information Technology Act and relevant rules governing personal data in India.

Developer Agreement

Developers retain all intellectual property rights in the software they submit to the indapp store. By listing an application on Qorelysofts, developers grant us a non-exclusive license to distribute the approved binary package and related assets through our platform.

Qorelysofts enforces a zero-bloatware and zero-malware policy. We require all applications to comply with our distribution standards and reserve the right to remove any utility that violates these terms, introduces unwanted software, or poses security risks to users.

Grievance Redressal

Qorelysofts maintains a formal grievance redressal process as required by Indian regulatory standards. Complaints and concerns submitted through the designated channel are acknowledged within 24 hours and managed with priority to ensure transparent resolution.

Our grievance mechanism is designed to address service issues, content concerns, and developer disputes in accordance with IT Rules 2021. We strive to resolve matters within 15 days from the date of receipt, with escalation pathways for unresolved cases.
